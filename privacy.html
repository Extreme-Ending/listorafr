Privacy Policy

Listora is an Etsy listing SEO audit tool. This policy explains what information is collected when you use the service and how it is handled.

Information collected

When you request an audit, Listora collects the Etsy listing URL you submit and, optionally, your email address to deliver your results. Listora uses the Etsy Open API v3 to retrieve public listing fields for the purpose of generating your audit: listing title, tags, description, category, price, and image count. All of these fields are publicly visible on Etsy.com. No payment data, private shop analytics, buyer information, or order data is ever accessed.

How information is used

Your listing URL and any retrieved listing data are used only to produce your SEO audit report. If you provided an email address, your results are sent to that address. Listora does not sell, share, or redistribute listing data or personal information to any third party for commercial purposes.

Service providers

Listora uses third-party services to operate, including cloud hosting, database storage, an AI recommendation layer (Anthropic Claude API), email delivery, and payment processing (Stripe). These providers process data only as needed to deliver the service.

Data retention

Audit data is retained while your account is active. If you did not create an account, data associated with your email is deleted after 90 days. You can request deletion at any time by contacting us.

Listora is an independent tool and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or certified by Etsy, Inc. "Etsy" is a trademark of Etsy, Inc.
